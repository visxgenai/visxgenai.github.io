Our Vision

VISxGenAI Vision

The VISxGenAI workshop aims to bridge the gap between visualization research and generative AI techniques. Our vision is to create a platform where researchers and practitioners can share innovative approaches to explaining and visualizing AI methods and results.

Goals

  • Foster collaboration between visualization and AI communities
  • Promote development of interactive, explainable AI visualizations
  • Showcase cutting-edge research on AI visualization techniques
  • Establish best practices for visual explanations of AI models

Impact

By emphasizing visual, interactive explanations of AI techniques, we aim to make complex AI systems more accessible, transparent, and trustworthy for all stakeholders, from researchers and developers to end-users and policymakers.

"Our mission is to advance the state-of-the-art in explainable AI through innovative visualization techniques, making AI more interpretable and accessible to everyone."
