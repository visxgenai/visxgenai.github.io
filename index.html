About Me

I'm a research scientist focusing on making machine learning more interpretable through visualization and interactive systems. My work combines techniques from deep learning, human-computer interaction, and information visualization.

Research Philosophy

I believe in creating tools and visualizations that help people understand complex systems. My research aims to bridge the gap between powerful ML models and human understanding.

Impact

By emphasizing visual, interactive explanations of AI techniques, we aim to make complex AI systems more accessible, transparent, and trustworthy for all stakeholders, from researchers and developers to end-users and policymakers.
